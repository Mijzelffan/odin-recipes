Ice water

A tasty cold brew.

A glass of water is just a plain old glass of water. As long as people have had glasses, they have put water in it.

However now we're really going to spice things up, and add ice to this glass of water.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • a glass
  • 1-3 ice cubes

Steps:

  1. put your water in a glass
  2. add the ice cubes to it, one at a time.
  3. enjoy a refreshing glas on me
